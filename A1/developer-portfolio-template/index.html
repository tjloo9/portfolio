Giving out Food to Homeless Project

As the team leader of an assignment project associated with the MPU subject Penghayatan Etika dan Peradaban, I was given the opportunity to lead my groupmates in distributing food to the homeless. It was a humbling experience to witness firsthand the struggles of those who are less fortunate, and it reinforced my commitment to giving back to society. Through this project, I developed a greater sense of empathy and compassion towards the homeless community, and I learned the importance of effective communication and collaboration in achieving a common goal. Overall, this project was a valuable experience that not only contributed to my personal growth, but also made a positive impact on the community.
